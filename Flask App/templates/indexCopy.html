Welcome to SAFRAN FS Asset Tracker Database

We are introducing SAFRAN FS new asset tracker to employees. SAFRAN FS's old asset tracker had some kinks that we noticed needed to be fixed so SAFRAN has been working with a team from Iowa State's Database Management Systems class to bring a new and improved database design to our doorstep.

Over the next few weeks our team leaders and members of the Database Management Systems class will be working with everyone to learn how the system works and the very user-friendly UI they have so kindly developed.

We hope that you all will enjoy the new asset tracker for our company and are eager to hear everybody's feedback!

Thank you for sticking with us through this transition,
